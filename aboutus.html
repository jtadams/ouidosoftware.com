About Us

Ouido was created based on a simple idea... That Software should actually help you do your job, not get in the way. That's why, when we designed Ouido, we threw out conventional thinking, and looked for a new way to solve a problem that has been attempted many times before. Conventional wisdom suggests that a problem with many attempted solutions has probably been solved, but, we knew that if that were the case, people would "love" their software, but, most people merely "tolerate" their software because they don't believe there is a better solution. Ouido will change your mind.

Ouido has create a brand new paradigm in process management. No longer does ever deal have to "fit" into the pre-defined model, but, the process can change and adapt to meet the needs of your deal. It someething we call Non-Linear Process Management, and it allows Ouido to adapt to the what is really happening in your deal, in real time.

We all know that no two deals happen the same way, so why does most software force every deal through the same process?

Ouido's promise to you is that you will never have to fight the software to reflect what is actually happening in your deal.
