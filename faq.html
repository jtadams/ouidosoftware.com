One of the best features of Ouido is how it both keeps your assistant and you on the same page. Ouido's dashboards provide you the overview and focus you need, while also giving your assistant the step by step instructions that lead them through your deal flow automatically.

This means fewer "what do I do next?" questions, and more focus for both of you. Which is why we say that Ouido will become your assistant's new best friend.

Ouido Keeps everyone on the same page, and even trains your new agents

While Ouido is a great tool for individual agents, it is with teams where it really shines. With tools like our "Filter me" button agents can filter any dashboard down to those items that they need to focus on (tasks, deals, dates, etc.), and with another click of the button return to seeing the whole team's perspective.

With Ouido's agent color-coding, your office manager and transactions coordinators will love being able to quickly identify which agent's deals are closing, and who is behind on tasks.

Ouido's automation and unique approach to workflows can handle any process

That's a bold statement, but, it's true. Ouido's templating system is not only easy to use, it's incredibly powerful as well. So powerful, in fact, that we like to say you're only limited by your imagination.

With Ouido's advanced task scheduling you can ensure that tasks always happen at the appropriate time (e.g. 5 days before closing). Our unique task triggers can kick off the next step in your process, or even move your deal into the right phase. And, because Ouido lets you combine those two things, you can move into a new phase, and trigger a specific workflow. Imagine an offer is received and you want that to trigger a counter offer. With Ouido, simple create a task in your workflow that moves the deal to the Offer Negotiation phase, and triggers the Prepare Counter Offer workflow. Offer is being accepted, Create a task (e.g. "Accept Offer), that by-passes the Offer Negotiations phase entirely, and moves the deal into a Closing Prep workflow.
The capabilities of this approach make sure your process works exactly as YOU want it to.

If you're not sure, our Professional Services team can help build your process for you, no matter how complicated... at least they haven't been stumped yet!
